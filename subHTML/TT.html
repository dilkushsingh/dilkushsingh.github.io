Teliya Talab

home / tourism / teliya talab

Teliya Talab , is located at Mandsaur City. best time to visit this place is evening time. sunset was so beautiful and so satisfying. we had a great time in the lap of nature. so calm and peaceful place.


It is a lake located on the western corner of Mandsaur city. For local citizens, its is used as a picnic spot. At present it is nice place to visit for spending quality time in peaceful manner. Best time to visit is during sunrise or sunset. Birding is also good in this area. Their is further scope to develop its surrounding area and make it free from encroachments.


A beautiful lake in mandsaur city. You can go here for morning or evening walks. To feed food to fishes. well made walking path and cemented chairs to sit alone or with friends and enjoy the lake beauty and sunset also. in winter migrating birds comes here. in monsoon when the lake gets overflow one can enjoy a small waterfall near by. on can go on a long walk lakeside on the pathway made.


This place is very good choice for picnics. It is located in the western part of the city. Transporation is easily available here.
